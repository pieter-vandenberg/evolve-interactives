Building a total in a loop

The hard part of a loop is that things change between passes. total grows and the counter k ticks up on every iteration, but plain text only shows you the final answer. Step through one pass at a time and watch the state move.

scores = [61, 99, 72, 87, 80, 95]
total = 0
k = 0

for score in scores:
    k = k + 1
    total = total + score
